Monsters

How to Summon

During your turn, you can normal summon in attack mode or set one monster in face down defense mode.

If a monster is level 1-4, you can normal summon it without tributing. If it is 5-6, it requires one tribute. If it is 7+, you can summon it with two tributes.


Summoning Mechanics

Fusion Summon: Activate a fusion spell and take two monsters that are in the requirements of a fusion monster, send them to the grave, and summon a fusion monster from the extra deck
Ritual Summon: Activate a ritual spell, send monsters with levels equal to a ritual monster to the grave and summon the ritual monster from the hand.
Synchro Summon: Take one tuner and one or more non tuners with levels totaling equal to one of your synchro monsters level, send them to the grave and summon it from your extra deck.
XYZ Summon: Take two monsters with levels equal to the rank of one of your xyz monsters, put them under that xyz monster as material.
Link Summon: Take two or more monsters send them to the grave and summon a link monster with rating equal to the number sent.

Spells

Spell Types:

Normal: Can be activated during your turn only.

Quick-Play: Can be activated at any time during your turn or on your opponents if set the turn prior.

Field: Can be activated during your turn. Stays in the field zone only one on each field until removed.

Continuous: Can be activated during your turn. Stays on the field until removed.


Traps

Trap Types:

Normal: Can be flipped at any time after the turn set

Counter: Can be flipped at any time after the turn set. Only counter traps can respond to them.

Continuous: Can be activated at any time the turn after set. Will stay on the field until removed.


Other Zones

Graveyard: When a card is removed from the field, deck, or hand, it is sent to the graveyard unless specified.

Banished: The banished zone is a special zone that cards go to only if a card specifies it. Harder to interract with than graveyard cards.

Banished Face Down: The rarest zone for cards to go. Only the owner of the cards can see what they are and they almost cannot be interracted with.

Extra Deck: The extra deck consists of 0-15 monsters. These can be fusions, synchro, xyz, and/or links. Pendulum monsters go face up in it when destroyed.

Deck: The main deck consists of 40-60 cards. Each player draws five at the beginning of the game and one each consecutive turn. The player going first does not draw on their first turn.

Side Deck: An additional deck of 0-15 cards that can be exchanged with cards from the main or extra deck between games. The same amount added must be removed.
